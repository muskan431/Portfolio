Slide Decks & Workflow Mapping

Project Summary:

I created visually engaging slide decks and clear workflow diagrams to support institutional documentation, employee training, and strategic planning processes. These assets were designed to simplify complex procedures and enhance communication across teams.

Key Features:

  • Professional slide presentations for internal meetings, onboarding, and project overviews
  • Workflow diagrams illustrating step-by-step processes, system architecture, and data flows
  • Use of color-coded layouts, icons, and illustrations for better comprehension
  • Modular templates to allow quick edits and reuse for future documentation
  • Integration of infographics, timelines, and flowcharts to represent logic and structure effectively

Tools & Technologies Used:

  • Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides – for presentation decks
  • Lucidchart, Draw.io, Canva, Figma – for workflow diagrams and process flows
  • Adobe Illustrator – for custom icons and illustrations
  • Use of consistent typography, branding, and visual hierarchy for clarity

Impact & Outcome:

These materials improved team understanding, reduced onboarding time, and supported smooth communication of technical and administrative processes. The visual clarity and structured approach helped in aligning departments and enhancing documentation quality.
